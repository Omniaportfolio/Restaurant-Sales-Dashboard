# Restaurant-Sales-Dashboard
This project analyzes restaurant sales data using Tableau Public to identify  revenue trends, top products, cities, and payment methods.
## Dataset
The dataset contains sales transactions including dates, cities, products, 
payment methods, and revenue.

## Tools Used
- Tableau Public
- Data Visualization
- KPI Design

## KPIs
- Total Revenue
- Top Product Category
- Top City by Revenue

## Key Insights
- Revenue shows strong peaks indicating seasonality.
- Lisbon is the highest revenue-generating city.
- Beverages lead in product sales.
- Online orders and credit cards are the most common payment methods.
